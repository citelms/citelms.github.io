Work History

Six Flags

My first job that I worked besides mowing the lawn for my neighbor was Six Flags New England.
It was an ok job to me it was not really a fun job I just needed it so I could pay my car bills.
I worked there over the summer of 2011 in the entertainment department, so i worked with the costume characters every day.
It was to me just a job to get money and I did not really like it so i only worked there for one summer.

Lifecare Centers of Wilbraham

A few months after I stopped working at Six Flags I was offered a job working in the kitchen at a retirement/rehab center for elderly.
This job was much more fun for me because while I obviously needed to do the job I was able to talk to the people I worked with
because they did not need to keep quite since they were in character. I worked this job for a little over three years until one day I
realized that I needed to leave or I would be there for much longer than I ever wanted to be.

Aldenville Liquors

While I was working at Lifecare for a little over 6 moths I also had a second job working at Aldenville Liquor store. When I eventually
quit Lifecare I took up more hours at the store in order to fill the gap in hours so I could still pay my bills. I am still currently
At the package store however I will be leaving after I graduate because durring my final semester at college I accepted a job offer
at a computer company for when I have my degree.

Future

My plan is to work at the company I have lined up for when I graduate. I plan on working there for a while and I know I will learn
